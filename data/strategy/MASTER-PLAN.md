# SLO Spain Strategy: Master Operational Playbook

## Executive Summary

This document outlines the complete strategy for launching and operating a portfolio of 5 Self-Liquidating Offer (SLO) digital products targeting the Spanish market. The SLO model uses paid advertising (primarily Google Ads) to acquire customers at break-even or slight profit on the front end, then generates real profit through order bumps, upsells, and backend email sequences.

**The 5-Product Portfolio:**

| # | Product | Target Audience | Front-End Price | Break-Even Status |
|---|---------|----------------|-----------------|-------------------|
| 1 | Kit Fiscal del Autonomo | Spanish freelancers (3.4M+) | 19 EUR | Self-liquidating at front-end + bump |
| 2 | Oposiciones Planner | Public exam candidates (500K+/yr) | 15 EUR | Profitable at front-end + bump |
| 3 | IA Prompts Profesionales | Knowledge workers (2M+) | 17 EUR | Needs upsell to break even |
| 4 | Calculadora Inmobiliaria | Property buyers (400K+ transactions/yr) | 27 EUR | Needs upsell to break even |
| 5 | Marketing Pymes Kit | Small business owners (3M+) | 27 EUR | Needs upsell to break even |

**Core Thesis:** Spain has 3.4 million autonomos, over 500,000 annual oposiciones candidates, and 3 million+ PYMEs. These audiences have acute, time-sensitive pain points and actively search Google for solutions. Digital products priced between 9-27 EUR face minimal purchase resistance and can be delivered instantly with zero marginal cost.

**Year 1 Revenue Target (Base Case):** 127,440 EUR gross revenue / 38,232 EUR net profit across all 5 products.

## Infrastructure Stack

### Frontend (Landing Pages & Checkout)

**Primary:** Astro static sites deployed on Vercel
- Why Astro: Lightning-fast load times (critical for Spain's mobile-heavy traffic), SEO-friendly, cheap to host
- Each product gets its own subdomain or domain
- Vercel free tier supports all 5 sites easily (100GB bandwidth/month)

**Domains:**
| Product | Primary Domain | Alternative |
|---------|---------------|-------------|
| Kit Fiscal | kitfiscalautonomo.es | kitfiscal.es |

| Inmobiliaria | calculadorainmobiliaria.es | gastosvivienda.es |
| Marketing Pymes | marketingpymes.es | kitmarketingpyme.es |

### Payments

**Stripe** (primary payment processor)
- Stripe Spain: 1.5% + 0.25 EUR per transaction (European cards)
- Supports order bumps via Stripe Checkout custom fields
- Tax collection for IVA (21%) handled automatically with Stripe Tax
- Invoicing for digital products

### Email Marketing & Automation

**Tier 1 (Free, Months 1-3):** Systeme.io free plan
- 2,000 contacts
- Unlimited emails
- Basic automation sequences
- Sufficient for validation phase

**Tier 2 (Growth, Month 4+):** Brevo (formerly Sendinblue)
- Free: 300 emails/day (9,000/month)
- Starter: 25 EUR/month for 20,000 emails
- GDPR-compliant (French company, EU data hosting)
- Transactional + marketing emails in one platform

### Advertising

**Google Ads** (primary acquisition channel)
- Search campaigns targeting high-intent keywords
- Spain-only targeting
- Language: Spanish
- Device bid adjustments: Mobile +20% (Spain is heavily mobile)

**Facebook/Instagram Ads** (secondary, retargeting only initially)
- Retargeting visitors who did not purchase
- Lookalike audiences once 100+ purchases accumulated
- Budget: 20% of total ad spend maximum

### Analytics & Tracking

- Google Analytics 4 (GA4) on all sites
- Google Tag Manager for event tracking
- Stripe Dashboard for revenue metrics
- Custom Google Sheets dashboard pulling from all sources
- Facebook Pixel for retargeting audiences

### Hosting & Delivery

- Vercel for site hosting (free tier)
- Google Drive or Gumroad for digital product delivery
- Stripe Checkout redirect to thank-you page with download links
- Backup delivery via automated email with download link

## Risk Mitigation

**Risk 1: CPC higher than projected**
- Mitigation: Start with exact match keywords only, add negative keywords aggressively, improve Quality Score through landing page relevance
- Kill switch: If CPA exceeds 2x front-end price after 200 EUR spend, pause and reassess

**Risk 2: Landing page conversion below 2%**
- Mitigation: A/B test 3 headline variants, add social proof (testimonials, media logos), add urgency (limited time pricing)
- Kill switch: If below 1.5% after 500 unique visitors, rebuild page from scratch

**Risk 3: Order bump take rate below 20%**
- Mitigation: Test different bump offers, test pricing, test copy on bump offer
- Note: Even at 20% bump rate, Kit Fiscal and Oposiciones remain profitable

**Risk 4: Stripe account issues**
- Mitigation: Keep refund rate below 1%, respond to all disputes within 24 hours, use clear product descriptions
- Backup: PayPal as secondary processor

**Risk 5: Seasonal demand drops**
- Mitigation: Diversified portfolio covers different seasons (fiscal = quarterly, oposiciones = spring/fall, inmobiliaria = spring, AI = evergreen)
- Plan: Reduce ad spend during low seasons rather than maintaining constant budget

## Decision Framework

**Scale a product when:**
- Front-end ROAS is above 0.8 (meaning upsell makes it profitable)
- CPA is within 20% of projection after 50+ conversions
- Order bump take rate is above 25%
- Upsell conversion is above 8%
- Customer complaints are below 2%

**Pause a product when:**
- Front-end ROAS is below 0.5 after 200+ clicks
- Refund rate exceeds 10%
- CPA is more than 2x projection after 300 EUR spent
- Landing page conversion stays below 1% after 3 A/B tests

**Kill a product when:**
- Cannot achieve 1.5% landing page conversion after full page rebuild
- CPC is 3x higher than projected with no path to reduction
- Product receives consistently negative feedback
- Market validation shows insufficient search volume (below 1,000 monthly searches for core keywords)
