## SLO Economics

### Target Scenario (EUR 27 front-end)
- Front-end: EUR 27
- Order bump (45% take rate): +EUR 7.65
- Upsell (20% take rate): +EUR 13.40
- **Average Customer Value: ~EUR 48**
- Target CPA (long-tail keywords): EUR 25
- **Profit per acquisition: ~EUR 23** (before email backend)

### Key Metrics Targets
- CTR: >2% | CPC: <EUR 2.00 | Conversion: >3% | Bump rate: >30% | ROAS: >1.5

## Platform

**Systeme.io** (Startup plan, $27/mo) -- chosen for:
- All-in-one: pages + checkout + bumps + upsells + email
- EU data hosting (AWS Ireland) for GDPR
- Native Spanish support
- EUR currency, 0% transaction fees

## Implementation Timeline

| Phase | Days | What |
|-------|------|------|
| Product Creation | 1-3 | Write prompts, guide, order bump, upsell outline |
| Funnel Build | 4-5 | Systeme.io: pages, checkout, bumps, upsells, emails |
| Google Ads Launch | 6-7 | Campaign setup, tracking, launch |
| Optimize | Week 2-4 | A/B test, scale winners, kill losers |
